## 📽️ Production Notes

### Camera Angles
- **Full Screen (Main)**: For the high-fidelity UI transitions.
- **Split Screen (Vertical)**: Terminal (Left) / SuiScan (Right) during the live bidding sequence.
- **Picture-in-Picture**: Keep a small 'Activity Feed' visible in the corner during the whole demo.

### Visual Effects
- **Glow**: Use a subtle orange glow transition when switching tabs.
- **Zoom**: Punch in on transaction hashes and blob IDs (don't make the judges squint).
- **Sound**: Simple, high-tech ambient background (minimal).

### Color Palette
- **Primary**: #F97316 (Sui Orange)
- **Secondary**: #3B82F6 (Testnet Blue)
- **Neutral**: #1C1917 (Stone-950)
